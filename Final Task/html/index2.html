    • Sustainable development goals that will be achieved

    1- Preserving natural resources: By using renewable energy sources such as solar and wind energy, it is possible to reduce dependence on fossil fuels and thus reduce harmful emissions to the environment and reduce unsustainable consumption of natural resources.
    2- Providing job opportunities: Implementing a renewable energy project in the countryside can lead to providing local job opportunities, whether in the construction and installation of the project or in its operation and maintenance.
    3- Improving the economic situation: Implementing a renewable energy project can improve the economic situation of the local community by providing job opportunities and enhancing economic sustainability.
    4- Improving health and the environment: The use of renewable energy can contribute to improving the health of individuals and reducing environmental pollution and negative impacts on the environment.
    5- Promoting local development: Implementing a renewable energy project can contribute to enhancing local development and providing opportunities for sustainable growth for the local community by improving its infrastructure and economy.
